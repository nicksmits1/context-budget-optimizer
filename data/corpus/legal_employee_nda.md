# Employee NDA and Intellectual Property Agreement

## Confidential Information

During your employment, you will have access to confidential information including but not limited to: source code, algorithms, customer data, financial information, business strategies, product roadmaps, and proprietary technology.

You agree to hold all confidential information in strict confidence and not to disclose it to any third party without prior written consent from the company. This obligation survives termination of employment for a period of two (2) years.

## Intellectual Property Assignment

All inventions, discoveries, designs, code, and other intellectual property created during your employment, using company resources, or related to the company's business, are the exclusive property of the company.

You agree to promptly disclose all such intellectual property and to execute all documents necessary to perfect the company's ownership rights.

## Exceptions

This agreement does not apply to:
- Inventions developed entirely on your own time without using company equipment or resources, unrelated to the company's business
- Open source contributions made to projects unrelated to company business (with prior manager approval)
- Prior inventions listed in Exhibit A (to be completed at onboarding)

## Non-Solicitation

For 12 months following termination, you agree not to solicit or hire any employee of the company, or solicit any customer or prospect with whom you had material contact during your last 12 months of employment.

## Return of Materials

Upon termination, you agree to return all company property including laptops, documents, access badges, and to delete all company data from personal devices. IT will provide a checklist and schedule a return appointment.

## Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California, without regard to conflict of laws principles.
