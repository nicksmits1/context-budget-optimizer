# 2025 Compensation Bands

## Philosophy

We target the 75th percentile of market compensation for all roles. Compensation data is sourced from Radford, Levels.fyi, and Pave. Bands are reviewed annually in January.

## Engineering

| Level | Title | Base Salary Range | Equity (4yr) |
|-------|-------|------------------|--------------|
| E3 | Software Engineer | $140K - $175K | $80K - $140K |
| E4 | Senior Engineer | $175K - $220K | $140K - $240K |
| E5 | Staff Engineer | $220K - $270K | $240K - $400K |
| E6 | Principal Engineer | $260K - $310K | $400K - $600K |
| M4 | Engineering Manager | $190K - $240K | $180K - $300K |
| M5 | Senior EM / Director | $240K - $290K | $300K - $500K |

## Product

|-------|-------|------------------|--------------|

| P4 | Senior PM | $170K - $215K | $120K - $220K |

## Sales

| Level | Title | Base (OTE) | Equity (4yr) |
|-------|-------|-----------|--------------|
| S3 | Account Executive | $80K ($160K) | $40K - $80K |
| S4 | Senior AE / Enterprise AE | $100K ($220K) | $80K - $160K |
| S5 | Regional Director | $130K ($280K) | $160K - $280K |

## General Policies

- Annual merit increases: 3-8% based on performance rating
- Promotion raises: minimum 10% base increase
- Equity refresh grants issued annually based on performance and tenure
- All employees receive benefits worth approximately $18K/year (health, dental, vision, 401K match, etc.)
- Remote employees receive the same compensation regardless of location (no geo-adjustment)
