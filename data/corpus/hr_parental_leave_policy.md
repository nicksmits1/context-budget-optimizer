# Parental Leave Policy

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have been with the company for at least 6 months are eligible for parental leave. This policy applies to birth parents, adoptive parents, and foster parents regardless of gender.

## Leave Duration

- **Primary caregiver:** 16 weeks of fully paid leave
- **Secondary caregiver:** 8 weeks of fully paid leave
- Employees may designate themselves as primary or secondary caregiver regardless of gender

Leave may be taken consecutively or split into two blocks within the first 12 months following the birth or placement of a child. If splitting leave, the minimum block duration is 2 weeks.

## Benefits During Leave

All benefits continue during parental leave, including health insurance, dental, vision, and 401(k) employer matching. Equity vesting continues uninterrupted.

Employees on parental leave remain eligible for company-wide bonuses and merit increases that occur during their leave period.

## Returning to Work

Employees are guaranteed the same or an equivalent role upon return. A flexible return-to-work program is available, allowing employees to work 60-80% hours for up to 4 weeks after returning, with full pay.

Managers should schedule a return-to-work meeting at least 2 weeks before the employee's planned return date to discuss any team or project changes.

## How to Apply

1. Notify your manager and HR at least 30 days before your expected leave start date (or as soon as practicable for adoption/foster placements)
2. Complete the Parental Leave Request form in Workday
3. HR will confirm your leave dates and benefits continuation within 5 business days

## Additional Support

- Backup childcare benefit: up to 10 days/year of subsidized emergency childcare
- New parent support group meets monthly (optional, see #parents Slack channel)
- Lactation rooms available on floors 3 and 5 of the main office

Questions? Contact hr@company.com or your HR Business Partner.
