# Q2 2025 Financial Report

## Revenue Summary

Total revenue for Q2 2025 was $41.4M, a 12% increase from Q1 2025 ($36.9M). Year-over-year growth was 28% compared to Q2 2024 ($32.3M).

Recurring revenue hit $31.6M (76.3% of total). The remaining $9.8M came from professional services, implementation, and training engagements.

## Segment Performance

**Enterprise:** $25.2M — Landed two new enterprise logos including a major financial services firm. Pipeline for Q3 looks strong with several Fortune 500 prospects in late-stage negotiations.

**Mid-Market:** $11.5M — Retention improved slightly to 91%. The product-led growth motion is starting to gain traction with 15 self-serve conversions.

**SMB:** $4.7M — Flat quarter-over-quarter. High churn in this segment (82% retention) remains a concern. CAC payback period for SMB is 18 months vs 10 months for mid-market.

## Cost Structure

Total operating expenses were $50.8M. The largest cost centers were R&D ($22.1M), Sales & Marketing ($18.3M), and G&A ($10.4M). We hired 34 people in Q2, primarily in engineering and customer success.

Gross margin was 69.8%, slightly below our 70% target. We expect this to improve in Q3 as the infrastructure migration completes.

## Cash and Runway

Cash position: $88.5M. Monthly burn: $5.1M. The board discussed the possibility of a down-round if market conditions don't improve, though current projections suggest we can reach profitability without additional funding if growth holds.
