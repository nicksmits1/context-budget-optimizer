# 2025 Annual Budget

## Revenue Plan

Total revenue target for 2025: $170M (base) to $175M (stretch).

| Quarter | Revenue Target | Cumulative |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| Q1 | $36.5M | $36.5M |
| Q2 | $40.0M | $76.5M |
| Q3 | $45.0M | $121.5M |
| Q4 | $51.0M | $172.5M |

Revenue growth is back-loaded due to expected enterprise deal closings in Q3-Q4 and the launch of the Context Budget API which is expected to drive expansion revenue.

## Expense Budget

Total operating expenses: $185M

| Category | Budget | % of Total |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| R&D | $82M | 44% |
| Sales & Marketing | $62M | 34% |
| G&A | $41M | 22% |

### R&D Breakdown
- Engineering salaries & benefits: $65M (target: 180 engineers by year-end, currently 142)
- Infrastructure (AWS): $12M (target 15% reduction from multi-tenant migration)
- ML/AI model costs: $3M (embedding inference, LLM API calls)
- Tools & licenses: $2M

### Sales & Marketing Breakdown
- Sales team comp (base + commissions): $38M
- Marketing programs: $14M (events, content, paid acquisition)
- Customer success: $10M

## Capital Allocation Priorities

1. Hire 40 engineers (focus: platform, ML, and security)
2. SOC 2 certification ($200K in audit costs)
3. International expansion research ($150K)
4. Office lease renewal in SF ($2.4M annual)

## Path to Profitability

At current growth rates and with planned expense discipline, we project EBITDA breakeven in Q2 2026. This assumes no additional funding round. If growth accelerates beyond $175M, the board has approved pulling forward the Series C to fund faster international expansion.
