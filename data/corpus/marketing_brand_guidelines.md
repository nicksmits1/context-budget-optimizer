# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Voice

Our brand voice is: **knowledgeable, approachable, and precise.** We're experts who don't talk down to people. We explain complex technical concepts clearly without oversimplifying.

**Do:** Use active voice, be specific with numbers and claims, lead with customer value
**Don't:** Use buzzwords without substance, make vague claims about AI, use fear-based messaging

## Visual Identity

### Logo
- Primary: Dark logo on light backgrounds
- Inverted: White logo on dark backgrounds
- Minimum clear space: 1x the height of the logomark on all sides
- Never stretch, rotate, or recolor the logo

### Colors
- Primary Blue: #2563EB
- Dark Navy: #1E293B
- Accent Green: #10B981
- Background Light: #F8FAFC
- Text Primary: #0F172A

### Typography
- Headlines: Inter Bold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Code: JetBrains Mono

## Messaging Framework

### Elevator Pitch (10 seconds)
"We connect your company's data to any AI tool, so your team gets answers grounded in your actual knowledge — not hallucinations."

### Value Proposition (30 seconds)
"Enterprise teams use dozens of tools, and critical knowledge is scattered everywhere. We create a unified context layer that connects to your existing data sources and serves the right information to AI assistants, copilots, and agents — with enterprise-grade permissions and optimization. Think of it as your company's memory, available everywhere."

### Positioning Statement
For enterprise technology leaders who need to make AI tools actually useful with company-specific knowledge, our platform is the context management layer that connects, optimizes, and serves enterprise data to any AI application, unlike generic search tools that treat all information equally.

## Content Guidelines

- Blog posts: 800-1,500 words, technical but accessible, always include a practical takeaway
- Case studies: Problem → Solution → Results format, include at least 2 quantitative metrics
- Social media: Focus on LinkedIn and Twitter/X, post 3-5x per week, mix of product updates, industry insights, and thought leadership
