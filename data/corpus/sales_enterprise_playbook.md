# Enterprise Sales Playbook

## Target Profile

Our ideal enterprise customer is a company with 1,000+ employees that uses multiple SaaS tools and has a growing AI/ML initiative. Key verticals: financial services, healthcare, technology, and professional services.

**Qualification Criteria (BANT):**
- Budget: $200K+ annual software spend on productivity/AI tools
- Authority: VP-level or above champion, ideally in Engineering or IT
- Need: Pain around knowledge silos, context switching, or AI adoption
- Timeline: Active evaluation within 6 months

## Sales Process

### Stage 1: Discovery (1-2 weeks)
Run a "Context Audit" with the prospect. Map out their data sources, current AI tool usage, and pain points around information retrieval. Use the Context Audit Template in Salesforce.

### Stage 2: Technical Evaluation (2-4 weeks)
Provide a sandbox environment. The SE team runs a proof-of-concept connecting 2-3 of their actual data sources. Key success metric: demonstrate <500ms context retrieval from their own data.

### Stage 3: Business Case (1-2 weeks)
Build an ROI model using our Enterprise ROI Calculator. Typical value drivers:
- 2-3 hours/week saved per knowledge worker on information search
- 40% reduction in context switching between tools
- Faster onboarding for new hires (access to institutional knowledge)

### Stage 4: Procurement (2-6 weeks)
Navigate security review, legal review, and procurement. Have the Security Questionnaire pre-filled. Engage our legal team early if DPA negotiations are needed.

## Competitive Positioning

Against Glean: We offer deeper AI integration and don't just search — we serve context directly into AI workflows. Against Guru: Our platform is API-first and built for developers, not just wiki-style knowledge management. Against in-house solutions: Building this internally typically takes 6-12 months and requires dedicated ML engineering headcount.
