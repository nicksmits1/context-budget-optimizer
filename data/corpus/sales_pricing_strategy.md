# Pricing Strategy — 2025

## Current Pricing Model

We use a per-seat pricing model with three tiers:

| Tier | Monthly Price/Seat | Target Segment |
|------|-------------------|----------------|
| Starter | $15/seat | SMB (10-100 employees) |
| Business | $25/seat | Mid-Market (100-1000 employees) |
| Enterprise | $35/seat | Enterprise (1000+ employees) |
| Enterprise Plus | $55/seat | Large Enterprise with custom needs |

Minimum contract: Starter/Business — monthly; Enterprise — annual ($100K minimum ACV).

## Pricing Philosophy

We price based on value delivered, not usage. Per-seat pricing is predictable for customers and aligns with our land-and-expand motion. We intentionally don't charge per query or per document to avoid creating disincentives to use the product.

Exception: the Enterprise Plus tier includes usage-based pricing for API calls above the included quota (10,000 context retrievals per seat per month). Overage rate: $0.01 per additional retrieval.

## Discounting Policy

- Multi-year commitment: 10% (2-year), 15% (3-year)
- Volume: 5% for >500 seats, 10% for >2,000 seats
- Maximum total discount: 25% (requires VP Sales approval)
- No discounts for monthly plans

## Competitive Pricing Analysis

| Competitor | Pricing Model | Comparable Price |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------|
| Glean | Per-seat | $30-40/seat (estimated, not public) |
| Guru | Per-seat + features | $15-25/seat |
| Dashworks | Per-seat | $20/seat (estimated) |

We are competitively positioned. The premium over Guru is justified by our AI-native architecture and API. The pricing is similar to Glean but with more transparent tiers.

## 2025 Pricing Changes Under Consideration

1. **Usage-based add-on:** Charge separately for the Context Budget API as a premium feature. Estimated price: $5/seat/month add-on. Concern: could slow adoption of our key differentiator.
2. **Freemium tier:** Offer a free tier with limited data sources (2) and seats (5) to drive PLG adoption. Expected cost: $50K/month in infrastructure for free users.
3. **Platform fee:** For the connector marketplace, take a 20% revenue share from third-party connector sales.

Board decision expected in Q3.
