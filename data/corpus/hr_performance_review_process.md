# Performance Review Process

## Overview

We run formal performance reviews twice per year in April and October. Reviews inform compensation adjustments, promotions, and development planning.

## Timeline

### April Cycle (H1 Review)
- March 15: Self-reviews open in Lattice
- March 25: Self-reviews due, peer review requests sent
- April 1: Peer reviews due
- April 1-10: Manager reviews and calibration
- April 11-15: Calibration sessions by department
- April 16-30: Manager delivers feedback in 1:1s
- May 1: Compensation adjustments effective

### October Cycle (H2 Review)
Same timeline, shifted by 6 months.

## Review Components

### Self-Review
Write 2-3 paragraphs covering: key accomplishments, areas for growth, and career goals. Reference specific projects and measurable outcomes where possible.

### Peer Reviews
Each employee selects 2-3 peers who can speak to their work. Peers provide feedback on collaboration, technical skill, and impact. Peer reviews are shared with the employee (not anonymous).

### Manager Review
Managers rate employees on a 5-point scale across four dimensions:
1. **Impact:** Did the work matter? Was it high quality?
2. **Execution:** Was work delivered on time and reliably?
3. **Collaboration:** How well does this person work with others?
4. **Growth:** Is this person developing their skills and scope?

Overall rating:
- **Exceptional (5):** Top 10% performer, significantly exceeds expectations
- **Strong (4):** Consistently exceeds expectations, ready for next level
- **Meeting (3):** Solid contributor, meets expectations
- **Developing (2):** Needs improvement in some areas
- **Below (1):** Not meeting expectations, PIP required

## Calibration

Department leaders meet to calibrate ratings and ensure consistency across teams. Target distribution: ~10% Exceptional, ~25% Strong, ~50% Meeting, ~15% Developing/Below. This is a guideline, not a forced ranking.

## Compensation Adjustments

| Rating | Merit Increase | Equity Refresh |
|--------|---------------|----------------|
| Exceptional | 6-8% | 1.5x standard |
| Strong | 4-6% | 1.0x standard |
| Meeting | 3-4% | 0.5x standard |
| Developing | 0% | None |
