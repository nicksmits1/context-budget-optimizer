# Remote Work Policy

## Overview

We are a remote-first company. All roles are open to fully remote work within the United States. We maintain offices in San Francisco and New York for those who prefer in-person work, but office attendance is not required for any role.

## Expectations

- **Core hours:** All employees should be available for synchronous communication between 10am-2pm PT on weekdays. Outside these hours, asynchronous communication via Slack and email is the norm.
- **Response times:** Slack messages should be acknowledged within 4 hours during business hours. Email within 24 hours.
- **Video on:** We encourage cameras on during meetings, but it's not mandatory. Cameras are expected for 1:1s and team meetings, optional for large all-hands.

## Equipment and Stipends

- New hire equipment: MacBook Pro (or equivalent), monitor, keyboard, mouse shipped to your home
- Home office stipend: $1,500 one-time setup allowance for desk, chair, etc.
- Monthly internet stipend: $75/month
- Coworking space: $200/month reimbursement for coworking membership if desired

## Team Gatherings

Each team has a budget for one in-person offsite per quarter (typically 2-3 days). The company hosts an all-hands week once per year, usually in San Francisco in September.

## International Remote Work

Extended international remote work (>2 weeks) requires approval from your manager and HR due to tax and compliance implications. We currently support remote work from Canada, UK, and Germany in addition to the US.

## Communication Norms

- Default to public Slack channels over DMs for work discussions
- Use threads to keep channels organized
- Record all meetings and post notes in the relevant Slack channel or Confluence page
- When in doubt about sync vs async, default to async
